About Florida

Florida is one of the most popular destinations people like to visit. Millions of people visit Florida yearly for a surplus of reasons. Miami is the second most visited city in the United States that offers authentic Latino culture while you enjoy tasty food and beautiful beaches. Orlando is the fourth most visited city and home to Disney World and Universal Studios, two of the largest theme parks in the world. The Everglades and the Florida Keys are also great areas to visit in Florida. Some like to visit Florida for the beaches, resorts, and hotels, some for the theme parks and nature. Whether you want to relax by the beach in Miami or explore the Florida Keys, the state of Florida has something for everyone.
